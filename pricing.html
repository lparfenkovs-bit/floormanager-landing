FAQ

Clear answers before the first live run.

Can a workstation skip ahead?

No. Routing executes in strict sequence, and Saw must finish before the next workcenter can start.

Do operators need admin access?

No. Costing and Settings stay admin-only, while operators receive the permissions needed to start timers and move jobs.

How is stock updated?

Materials deduct automatically when the work order completes, regardless of operator inventory permissions.
